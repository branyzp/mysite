Pomodoro Timer

The Pomodoro (Italian for tomato) technique was developed in the late 1980s by Francesco Cirillo during his university days.
He used his tomato shaped kitchen timer to commit 10 minutes of study time and found that it worked fantastically well. This popular time management method uses focused sessions of work with frequent short breaks to stave off mental fatigue.
